University Admissions

We specialise in Oxbridge applications, personal statement guidance, entrance exams and interview coaching. As we are all current Oxford students who went through the application process only a few years ago, we are well aware of what it entails. We know what kind of preparation is useful, and what isn't. In the medium-term, we can assess a student's strengths and weaknesses through one-on-one sesssions with our Senior Tutors and give them advice on what areas to focus on and what the most effective ways of doing so are. As part of this medium-term preparation, we can give students reading lists, marked writing assignments, preparation lessons for course-specific exams like the TSA and PAT (some of which are also used by other top universities, including the London School of Economics). We also give advice on how to most efficiently manage their A-level work whilst simultaneously preparing for university entrance exams. When it comes to short-term preparation, we do mock interviews and confidence building sessions to help students perform at their best.

Scholarship Preparation

We help students prepare for competitive scholarship applications and entrance examinations. Our past students include some who have won King's Scholarships to Eton college, and many more who successfully sat the entrance exams to other top public schools like Winchester, Harrow, and St Paul's. We have also successfully prepared students who went on to win scholarhsips to excellent Australian private schools like Haileybury College and Wesley College.
