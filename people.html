Harvey Garnaut

Hi! I'm a PPE student at Balliol College, Oxford. I graduated high school only a few years ago, so don't worry, I can definitely remember the stress of tests, homework and A-levels. I'll still be challenging you, though! I teach all the way from primary school to sixth form, and occasionally tutor university students as well.
