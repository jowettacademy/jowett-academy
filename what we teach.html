What We Teach

University Admissions

We provide tailored support for students applying to universities, including guidance on choosing the right course, help with personal statements, and interview preparation. We also help students prepare for Oxbridge admissions tests, inlcuding the TSA, TMUA, PAT, MLAT, BMSAT and UCAT.

A Levels

Between us, we teach Maths, Further Maths, Chemistry, Physics, Biology, Economics, Politics, Philosophy, English, History, French, Italian, Latin and Greek. Not only do our tutors study these subjects at university, they all received top marks in their A Levels in these subjects and so know exactly what the examiners are looking for.

VCE

For students undertaking the Victorian Certificate of Education (VCE), we provide targeted teaching that builds both subject mastery and assessment skills, helping students achieve their personal best and open doors to their chosen pathways.

GCSE

Our GCSE programs focus on building strong foundations and confidence in each subject. We equip students with the knowledge, study skills, and exam strategies needed to excel in their GCSE examinations.

Scholarship Preparation

We help students prepare for competitive scholarship exams with rigorous teaching, practice assessments, and coaching designed to bring out their strengths and showcase their abilities to scholarship boards.

Lower Years

For younger students, we nurture curiosity and a love of learning from the start. Our teaching builds strong academic habits and foundational knowledge that set students up for future success.
